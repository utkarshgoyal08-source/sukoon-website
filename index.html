Our Story

Two Friends, One Passion

At Sukoon, we pour our passion for artistry and craftsmanship into each candle, meticulously crafted to bring a touch of calm and elegance into your home.

As two friends fueled by passion, we embarked on this journey to share our love for home decor with the world. Each piece is lovingly crafted by hand, celebrating the imperfections that make handmade products truly one-of-a-kind. From soothing scents to unique designs, our pieces blend tradition with modern flair, adding warmth and tranquility to your space.
